#+TITLE: Union Budget
#+FILETAGS: :india:politics:government:


* main
- annual financial statement
- statement of estimated receipts and expenditure of govt for a particular year
- account of govt's finances for [[file:fiscal_year.org][fiscal year]] from 1st April to 31st march.

* classification
** Revenue Budget
- govt's revenue reciepts and expenditure
- revenue receipts -> tax and non tax
- revenue expenditure -> expenditure incurred in day to day functioning of govt on services offered to citizens
- if revenue expenditure > revenue receipts -> revenue deficit

** Capital Budget
- capital receipts and payments of govt
- loans from public, foreign govts + RBI => major part of CB
